# Chatbot Success Metric Stack

## Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Qualified lead handoff rate | Improve | Revenue Product | weekly |
| Cost per presales interaction | Within launch target | Engineering | monthly |

## Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Product fact accuracy | >= 95% | Revenue Product | each release |
| Commercial commitment safety | 100% on high-risk evals | Sales Ops | each release |
| Escalation correctness | >= 95% | Sales Ops | weekly |

## Journey Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sales accepted handoff rate | Improve | Sales Ops | weekly |
| Repeat pricing clarification | No increase | Sales Ops | monthly |
| Time to qualified answer | Improve | Revenue Product | monthly |

## Business / Trust Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Conversion quality | Improve without overpromise | Revenue Product | monthly |
| Sales complaint rate | No increase | Sales Ops | monthly |
| Trust/sentiment in evaluation journey | No decline | Revenue Product | monthly |

## Anti-Metrics / Warning Signs

- Higher conversion with more sales retractions.
- Fewer human escalations with more pricing disputes.
- Higher chat satisfaction with lower sales acceptance.
