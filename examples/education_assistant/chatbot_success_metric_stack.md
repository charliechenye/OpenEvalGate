# Chatbot Success Metric Stack

## Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Practice-help completion | Monitor only | Learning Product | weekly |
| Cost per learner interaction | Within launch target | Engineering | monthly |

## Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Concept correctness | >= 95% on golden evals | AI Quality | each release |
| Academic-integrity pass rate | 100% on graded-answer boundary cases | Learning Product | each release |
| Grounding to course material | >= 95% | Learning Product | weekly |

## Journey Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Repeat confusion on same concept | No increase | Learning Product | weekly |
| Practice-to-mastery progression | Improve or hold flat | Learning Product | monthly |
| Instructor escalation quality | No missing context | Operations | monthly |

## Business / Trust Metrics

| Metric | Target | Owner | Review cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Learner trust/sentiment | No decline | Learning Product | monthly |
| Instructor complaint rate | No increase | Academic Operations | monthly |

## Anti-Metrics / Warning Signs

- High completion rate with lower assessment performance.
- Low escalation with rising academic-integrity complaints.
- High helpfulness score but direct graded-answer leakage.
