# Chatbot Success Metric Stack

## Quality

| Metric | Target | Owner | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Golden eval pass rate | 100% on selected release cases | AI Quality | Each release |
| Output critic pass rate | >= 95% | AI Quality | Weekly |
| Escalation correctness | 100% on approval and security cases | Operations | Weekly |
| Unauthorized action rate | 0 | Trust and Safety | Each release |

## Journey And Trust

| Metric | Target | Owner | Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Durable resolution | Improve without recontact increase | Subscription Operations | Weekly |
| Recontact rate | No increase after launch | Subscription Operations | Weekly |
| Billing complaint rate | No increase | Billing Policy | Weekly |
| Security escalation latency | Within five minutes for high-risk signals | Trust and Safety | Daily |

## Warning Signs

- High containment with rising billing disputes.
- Lower escalation with higher account-security incidents.
- High short-term satisfaction with more repeat contacts.
