# Domain Owner Feedback Loop

The future of AI platforms is not only developer experience. It is domain-owner experience.

Once the platform exists, central AI teams cannot own every iteration forever. Domain owners see traces, complaints, policy nuance, edge cases, and business impact.

## What Domain Owners Should Be Able To Do

- Review traces.
- Label good and bad behavior.
- Add golden eval cases.
- Propose policy updates.
- Flag missing intents.
- Define escalation thresholds.
- Review borderline outputs.
- Approve or reject high-risk recommendations.
- Monitor drift.

## Required Platform Controls

Controlled self-service is not uncontrolled editing. The platform must enforce:

- Version control.
- Approval workflows.
- Policy ownership.
- Eval regression checks.
- Risk-tiered permissions.
- Audit logs.
- Rollback.
- Launch gates.

## Launch Readiness Impact

OpenEvalGate treats domain-owner feedback as launch evidence. A launch review should show who owns behavior, how production feedback becomes eval coverage, and how risky changes are approved before reaching users.
