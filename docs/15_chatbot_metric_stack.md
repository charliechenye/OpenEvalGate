# Chatbot Metric Stack

Containment is not resolution.

A chatbot can improve containment while hurting customer trust. Single-session success can create journey-level failure. A customer may return later angrier, learn to bypass the bot, stop trusting automation, stop trusting the platform, or churn silently without CSAT.

Containment tells you whether the bot absorbed the session. Resolution tells you whether the customer's problem was solved. Trust tells you whether the platform relationship survived.

## Metric Layers

### Efficiency Metrics

- Containment.
- Deflection.
- Cost per interaction.
- Handle time.
- Automation rate.

### Quality Metrics

- Answer correctness.
- Grounding score.
- Policy alignment.
- Output critic pass rate.
- Escalation correctness.

### Journey Metrics

- True resolution.
- Recontact rate.
- Repeat escalation.
- Time to durable resolution.
- Bypass attempts.

### Business And Trust Metrics

- Retention.
- Repurchase.
- Lifetime value.
- Complaint rate.
- Trust or sentiment.
- Downstream support cost.

## Warning Signs

- High containment with high recontact.
- High automation with falling CSAT.
- Low escalation with rising complaints.
- High bot CSAT but lower repurchase.
- Fewer human contacts but higher churn.
- High output critic pass rate but poor journey resolution.
